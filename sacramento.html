The City of Sacramento

Sacramento, the capital city of California, is situated in the heart of the state's lush Central Valley. It's known for its rich history, vibrant cultural scene, and has many attractions ranging from the California State Capitol Building to the Crocker Art Museum. The city enjoys a Mediterranean climate, characterized by hot, dry summers and mild winters, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts.
